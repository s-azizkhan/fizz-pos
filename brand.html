04 — Lexicon · Why "Fizz" works

The sound is the strategy

"Fizz" is a near-total fricative — forced, continuous airflow. Phonosemantically that reads as speed, air, and effervescence. The front vowel "i" reads small, fast, sharp. And it's beverage-native without ever saying "POS" — it breaks every category code (no Pay-, no -Touch, no -Bistro).
